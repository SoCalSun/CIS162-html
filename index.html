About Me

Welcome to my webpage all about me, David Hall! Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by three things: the military, cars, and computers. After high school, I spent a few years living abroad. When I came back, I joined the Air Force and served for ten years, separating on Thanksgiving Day, 2016. Not long after, I became a mechanic and have been working in that field ever since.

I lived on the East Coast for the past 15 years, but I moved back home in February of this year. I left my management career in Maryland to dive into my third passion: computers. During my time in the military, I got a taste of information technology, but it wasn’t my main job. Now, I’m enrolled in a computer programming certificate program and loving it so far. I enjoy challenging work, creating things, and solving problems, which I believe this new career will offer.
